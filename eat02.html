Where to eat

MEXICAN

Fonda San Miguel - Upscale mexican dining in the heart of Austin. Good place to impress clients.

Maudie's - Several locations across Austin. This is a favorite of Austinites.

Güero’s Taco Bar - In the heart of South Congress, Güero’s is a great place to grab lunch while shopping.

Juan in a Million - Open daily from 7AM to 3PM it serves the best breakfast in town. Try the Migas.

Matt’s El Rancho - Fun place for enchiladas and margaritas.

Z’Tejas - Located on West 6th, it caters to a fancier clientele.

Polvos - The original location in south Austin is a fun place for dinner and drinks. Their new location downtown is a trendy spot too.

Curra’s Grill - Little hole-in-the-wall mexican restaurant. Try Curra's amazing avocado margarita! just trust me, you'll love it.
