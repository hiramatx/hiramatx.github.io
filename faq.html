Welcome to Austin! Here are a few things to know while you’re here

TEXAS BLUE LAWS

Austin has blue laws governing the sale of alcoholic beverages.

Liquor:

Is only sold at liquor stores. Liquor stores are open Mon-Sat from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM only! All liquor stores are closed on Sundays. Please make sure you plan ahead if you want to enjoy cocktails at home/hotel after hours. I recommend either Spec’s or Twin Liquors for your spirit needs

Beer/Wine:

Is sold at most convenience stores and Supermarkets. Sales are Mon-Fri 10:00AM to Midnight, Saturdays 10:00AM to 1:00AM.

Bars/Clubs:

Are open until 2:00 AM. Some bars close at Midnight on weekdays, so call ahead.

AUSTIN BATS

Austin has the largest urban bat population. One and a half million bats live under the Congress Avenue Bridge in Austin. If you haven’t taken a trip to watch them take flight, be sure to add it to your list! (You can see them from March through October.)

AUSTIN SPORTS TEAMS

Yes, we live and die for out UT Longhorns (Go Horns!). But did you know that we are now home to our first professional sports team? Austin FC is our brand new soccer team, check them out at austinfc.com

AUSTIN'S F1 RACETRACK

There is only one F1 racetrack in the US, and it's here in Austin. Circuit of the Americas (COTA) also hosts all sorts of other events, check out their calendar for more details
