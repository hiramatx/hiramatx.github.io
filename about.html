ABOUT ME

Hello and welcome!

I’m your driver, Hiram (Pronounced [HY]-[rum]). I go by “H” to most people.

I am Dominican and I grew up in The Bronx, NY. I have been married for 16 years and have 3 children. This is my only gig for right now since I’m a stay-at-home dad to my beautiful 3-year-old daughter.

I have been living in Austin for the past 14 years, and I’ve been driving for Uber for 5+ years, so I’m pretty familiar with the city and I’m full of recommendations for things to do here. I have shared some of them here for you. After driving visitors and new residents around my gorgeous city, I decided to make this site with the usual information I give my riders. I hope you have enjoyed your ride today!
